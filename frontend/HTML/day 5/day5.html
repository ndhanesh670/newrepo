About Chennai

Chennai is the capital of Tamil Nadu. It is known for Marina Beach and rich traditions.

The city is famous for filter coffee and classical music.

Chennai is located on the Bay of Bengal and has a long coastline. It is one of the major metropolitan cities in India.

The city experiences hot summers, moderate winters, and heavy rainfall during the monsoon season.

Chennai is also known for its strong cultural values, traditional festivals, and temple architecture.

IT Sector

Technology Growth

Chennai has many IT parks in OMR. Many software engineers work here.

OMR is also called the IT corridor of Chennai. Many startup companies are growing rapidly.

The IT industry provides jobs to thousands of young professionals.

Famous Companies

TCS, Infosys and other companies have offices in Chennai.

Many multinational companies have development centers here.

The IT sector contributes greatly to the economy of the city.

Tourism

Marina Beach is one of the longest beaches in the world. Many people visit Chennai for temples and beaches.

Kapaleeshwarar Temple and Santhome Church are popular tourist spots.

Fort St. George is an important historical place in Chennai.

Shopping malls, cinemas, and parks also attract visitors.

Cultural festivals and music concerts are conducted regularly.

Fun fact!

Chennai was earlier called Madras.

The name was officially changed in 1996.
