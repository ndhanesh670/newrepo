Once upon a time

firstt

I was 12, i saw my uncle snapping his fingers, i was fascinated by the way it looked and sounded.

So naturally i asked him to teach me, i wanted to learn it so bad.

He told me to put my middle finger and thumb finger together, put pressure on it the let it go! let the middle finger hit your hand.I tried to do it do it the way he told me, but younger me had a softer hand, so a crisp sound did not come out.


bit into the future

I remember being disappointed at the moment, then i gave up on it. After a few years, i tried it again, it worked! I was excited that i was able to do it and i moved on.
